Leona

80m Bilgin Yachts Masterpiece

The Red Bullet

Launched in 2023, the 80-meter Leona is the second hull in the Bilgin 263 series. Known affectionately as the "Red Bullet" due to her striking red-and-white hull, she represents the pinnacle of luxury and engineering. Her bullet-shaped profile by Unique Yacht Design ensures highly efficient cruising and an unforgettable silhouette.
