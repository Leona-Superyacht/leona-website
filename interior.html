Interior

Designed by H2 Yacht Design

Unapologetic Opulence

Leona's interior breaks away from contemporary minimalist trends. Designed by the renowned H2 Yacht Design, the living spaces are a masterclass in rich textures and bold statements. Featuring high-gloss lacquers, backlit tiger onyx, and intricate 18-carat gold detailing, the yacht feels like a floating palace.

One of the most notable layout choices is the reduction of guest cabins. Instead of the typical four cabins on the lower deck, Leona features only two massive VIP staterooms, prioritizing extraordinary volume and personal space for her guests.

The Iconic Beach Club

The crown jewel of Leona's interior is undeniably the beach club. Spanning a massive footprint, it is centered around an 8.7-meter mosaic pool flanked by classical Grecian statues pouring water into the basin.
