The Bridge

Command & Navigation Center

Integrated "Glass Bridge" System

Leona's command center is defined by a state-of-the-art Integrated Bridge System (IBS). Gone are the days of scattered analog dials; the helm is dominated by ultra-high-definition, multi-function touch displays. These panels consolidate radar, sonar, engine telemetry, and CCTV into a single, highly customizable interface.

This layout not only provides a sleek, futuristic aesthetic but also drastically improves situational awareness. The captain can configure the layout of the screens on the fly, instantly calling up critical data where and when it is needed most.

Advanced Navigation & Safety

Safety and precision are paramount for an 80-meter vessel. The bridge is equipped with dual Electronic Chart Display and Information Systems (ECDIS), entirely replacing paper charts. To cut through the darkest nights and heaviest weather, Leona utilizes both X-band and S-band radar systems, ensuring detection of both fine details nearby and large weather patterns at a distance.

Dynamic Positioning & Control

Maneuvering a 1,730 GT superyacht requires incredible precision. The bridge features an advanced Dynamic Positioning (DP) system that integrates GPS data with the yacht's twin MTU engines and heavy-duty bow thrusters.

With the push of a button, the captain can lock the vessel into an exact GPS coordinate.
